Roblox

Roblox is a online multiplayer game that allows users to design their own games and play a wide variety of different types of games created by users. One of my favorite game to play in Roblox is DTI or Dress To Impress. Dress To Impress is a game about showing your fashion creativity. In Dress To Impress you can show your artistic way of making a stylish oufits. By joining one of the themed contests you can earn stars and climb from the ranks "New Model" to "Top Model"

5 Reasons why this game is my favortite.

  • It is fun
  • The game is entertaining when choosing outfits to wear
  • It enhances my creativity skills
  • The game is amazing
  • It is good to compete with other users.
